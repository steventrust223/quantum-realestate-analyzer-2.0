‚ö° Key Features

MAO Calculation

Maximum Allowable Offer is calculated using:

  • ARV (After Repair Value) estimation
  • Repair costs (Light vs Full rehab)
  • Holding costs & closing costs
  • Target profit & assignment fees
  • Strategy-specific formulas (Wholesale, Sub2, Wrap)

Deal Classification

Properties are automatically classified into:

  • HOT DEAL: High profit, high margin, low risk
  • SOLID: Good profit, acceptable margin & risk
  • PORTFOLIO: Lower profit but stable/long-term potential
  • PASS: Doesn't meet minimum criteria

Exit Strategy Recommendations

System recommends the best exit strategy for each property:

  • Wholesale: Quick flip with assignment fee
  • Wholetail: Light repairs, fast sale
  • Sub2: Subject-to existing financing
  • Wraparound: Seller financing structure
  • STR/MTR/LTR: Short/mid/long-term rentals

Buyer Matching

Automatically matches properties to buyers based on:

  • Geographic preferences (ZIP, City)
  • Price range
  • Strategy preference
  • Property type & repair tolerance

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How accurate are the ARV estimates?
A: Currently, ARV is estimated at 115% of asking price as a conservative heuristic. For production use, integrate with Zillow API or use comp data for more accurate valuations.
Q: Can I customize the thresholds and formulas?
A: Yes! Go to Settings to adjust MAO percentages, profit targets, risk thresholds, and more. All calculations use these settings.
Q: How do I integrate with my CRM?
A: The system includes stub functions for CRM integration. Look for functions marked "STUB" in the code (e.g., RE_sendPropertyToBuyer). You can wire these to CompanyHub, SMS-iT, or other tools via their APIs.
Q: What's the difference between Sub2 and Wraparound?
A: Sub2 (Subject To): You take ownership subject to existing financing. Best for low-equity deals with good cash flow.
Wraparound: Seller carries financing while underlying loan stays in place. You make payments to seller, seller pays underlying loan. Good for moderate-equity deals.
Q: Can I delete leads or properties?
A: Yes, but be careful! If you delete from LEADS_* sheets, it won't affect MASTER_PROPERTIES (already imported). If you delete from MASTER_PROPERTIES, you'll lose all analysis data. Instead, consider changing the Status to "Dead" to keep history.
